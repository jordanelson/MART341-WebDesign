Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Cup Butter
  • 3/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Cups Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 375Ëš F.
  2. 2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt. In a seperate bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. 3. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto baking sheet.
  4. 4. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes until golden brown.
